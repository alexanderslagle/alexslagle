Playtesting

Alex conducted many playtests in the development of Lost Duckling. Playtesting started when the game was a paper prototype until the final build was ready to be released. Playtesting helped inspire new features, discover issues, and improve the game's usability.

Paper Prototypes

Alex started Lost Duckling as a paper prototype. Using a paper prototype allowed for rapidly testing new features without spending time implementing them in Unity; cutting down development time.
